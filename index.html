What is the ICH volume calculator?
The ICH volume calculator is a tool that determines the size of a parenchymal hemorrhage based on a CT scan.
How to calculate ICH volume?
To calculate ICH volume you should use the following formula:
ICH volume = (length * width * (slice thickness / 10) * number of slices) / 2
Why use ICH volume calculator?
The ICH volume calculator is used in order to calculate the size of a parenchymal hemorrhage based on a CT scan.
